My Journey

My journey into data science began during my final year at Vellore Institute of Technology, where I was working on a robotics capstone project that completely changed my perspective on technology.

What started as a mechanical engineering project quickly became my introduction to programming and data analysis. I found myself fascinated by how code could bring hardware to life and how data could guide intelligent decision making. This hands-on experience with robotics and programming opened my eyes to a whole new world of possibilities.

That robotics project was the spark that pushed me toward data science. I realized I wanted to work more with data and algorithms rather than just mechanical systems. After completing my bachelor's degree, I made the decision to pursue my Master's in Data Science at Vanderbilt University.

During my studies at Vanderbilt, I had incredible opportunities to apply what I was learning in real-world settings. I spent a summer at Stardust Materials working with molecular modeling , which gave me experience with high-performance computing and advanced data processing. Later, I worked on my capstone project with SymTrain, developing AI-powered systems for automating training simulations using computer vision, large language models and natural language processing.

Now that I've completed my Master's degree, I'm excited to bring this combination of engineering thinking, programming skills, and data science expertise to solve meaningful problems and create business impact. Every project I work on reinforces my belief that data, when approached thoughtfully, can drive incredible results.
